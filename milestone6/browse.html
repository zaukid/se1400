Word Crush

Brain crushing word game

$34.99

Ages: 8+ Number of Players: 2+ Playing Time: 20+ min Be the first to discard your whole hand to win this brain taxing memory game.... Take five minutes to learn the rules and set the game up. Then let the grey matter get its workout in. A good memory is helpful, but to win you'll need to plan ahead and make a few good strategic moves. Impress yourself and your friends by reciting a long list of random words. Or forget a single word and watch your hand grow and your mind shrivel. Players hold word cards in their hands which are played alphabetically as play proceeds. Each time a player adds to the string of previously played cards, he must recite the words on the cards beneath the one he's playing. But hold on! Words are not just alphabetical by their first letter. They can also be played if their word contains a double of the active letter. For example, if F is the active letter, FOGGY can be played, but so can CAFFEINE. Players who fail to correctly recite the word stack add any unrecited words to their own hands. Word Crush outcomes can become unpredictable and frustrating. Best laid plans can be laid to waste in a moment. One forgotten or misremembered word cam mean inevitable defeat. Extraordinarily competitive games can built word lists that cycle through the entire alphabet, and then some. When you win a game of Word Crush, you'll leave feeling like it was all you. Lady Luck had nothing to do with it.


Die Caster

A gambling type game

$34.99

Ages: 5+ Number of Players: 2+ Playing Time: 20+ min Hold the highest chip value at the end of the game to win this fast-paced, easy-to-learn game.... Die Caster is a casino Craps-style game with none of the complexity, the player elimination or camaraderie. Players are pitted against each other, but all complete against the roller. The rules are simple. After the roller's first two-dice roll, the other players wager whether his second roll will be higher or lower than his first. Winners are always paid what they wagered, and may change their bets right up to the moment the dice are rolled. If the roller throws two equal rolls, he wins instead and gets all the chips on the board. Play continues for a predetermined number of rounds, minutes, or maximum chip value. Die Caster is entertaining but easy to learn for young and old alike. Children are introduced to the concept of two dice roll probability. Trailing players are never truly out until they complete their final rolls. Lead changes can happen rapidly and unexpectedly! Get Die Caster for your game closet, but keep it on top! It takes only a minute or two to refreshen the rules if it's been a while since you last played. It's the perfect game for family gatherings or game nights with friends. Younger children can participate with minimal help from older siblings or parents.

Snake Eyes and Box Cars

Roll dice, score points, and outlast opponents

$9.99

Ages: 3+ Number of Players: 2-6 Playing Time: 10 min Roll a 7 when your score is 7, or be the last eliminated to win Snake Eyes & Box Cars.... This Yatzee!-style two-dice game pits players against each other in an elimination game of chance. There are only four easy rules to this game that result in scores between -2 & 4 each turn. Players are eliminated when they reach -10. They win by being the sole survivor or by rolling a 7 when their score is 7. Snake Eyes & Box Cars is easy to learn for young children, and is fun for the whole family. Games are quick and entertaining. Game form is small and perfect for travel.
