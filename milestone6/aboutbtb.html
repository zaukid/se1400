About Beyond the Board

Who We Are

Welcome to Beyond the Board! We are pleased you stopped by for a visit. BtB is a family- owned Utah-based board game company. We were created in 2014 by a family of board game enthusiasts with a dream of bringing our game ideas to the marketplace, and a vision of filling a void left by other creators. We welcome and appreciate customer feedback, whether it be good, bad, or indiﬀerent. If you’re just getting started with BtB, might we suggest taking a look at our best sellers: Word Crush, and Die Caster? We think you’ll be pleased.

Objective

Years ago, we at Beyond the Board recognized a void in the board game universe. Games which we found to be fun had steep learning curves and complex setups. The rules complexity meant that after keeping the game in our game closet for months (or even years), we had to overcome the learning curve again before we could play. This became frustrating very quickly, and sometimes we found ourselves putting the game back before even playing it. The complex game setups usually involved a lot of custom card decks, playing pieces, and game boards. The unavailability of game replacement parts meant that missing or damaged pieces rendered the game unplayable, or at least less enjoyable.

Our goal at BtB is to create truly entertaining and enjoyable games with low learning curves and few custom parts. We also strive to make custom parts available for purchase separately, so our customers can keep their games complete and in good, playable, condition. We want our games to be learnable in 5 minutes or less, and game setup to be achievable in 2 minutes or less. If our customers leave our game in their own game closets for years, we want them to be up-and-running in under 10 minutes.

We at BtB believe that board game play should be shared with family and friends. We try to oﬀer games that both young children and adults can enjoy together.
